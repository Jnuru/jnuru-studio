WHO I AM

A designer focused on clear websites that hold up.

I'm based in St. Louis and work where design, content, and website structure meet. By day I build digital experiences for Lou Fusz, a large family-owned automotive network based in St. Louis. Outside of that, I run jnuru.studio for small businesses that need a web presence they can trust and manage.

Outside the screen, I run. Ultras, marathons, early mornings, long miles. That discipline shows up in the work: patient decisions, clean structure, and no shortcuts.

Authenticity

Discipline
