WHO I AM

A designer focused on structure, not decoration.

I'm based in St. Louis and work at the intersection of design and systems thinking. By day I build digital experiences for one of the largest automotive groups in the country. Outside of that I run jnuru.studio — focused on small businesses that need a brand and web presence that actually holds up.

When I'm not designing I'm running — ultras, marathons, long miles in the early morning. That same discipline carries into the work. No shortcuts. No decoration for its own sake. Just things built to last.
