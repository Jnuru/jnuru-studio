PHILOSOPHY

Design is translation, not decoration.

Before layout or interaction, I look for what the work is trying to honor — the story, the intention, and the person behind it.

I’m not interested in visuals with no meaning. Clarity matters. Structure matters. Restraint matters.

  • What someone is trying to say before they know how to say it
  • The tension between vision and execution
  • The system that lets the story scale without losing soul

NOTE

Code serves the idea — never the other way around.

Calm interfaces. Strong structure. Editorial pacing. Subtle character.
