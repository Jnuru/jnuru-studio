QUESTIONS / 06

Straight answers before you ever email me.

How much does a website cost?

Most jnuru.studio projects land between $2,500 and $8,000. Focused launch sites start at $2,500, full redesigns and Shopify builds typically run $4,000 to $8,000, and larger scopes are quoted from $8,000 up. Every project starts with a written plan and a fixed price before any design begins, so the number you approve is the number you pay.

How long does a website redesign take?

Most redesigns take 3 to 6 weeks from blueprint to launch. The blueprint session happens in week one, you see real designed pages before anything is built, and you get a walkthrough at handoff so you can update the site yourself.

Do I need a redesign or a brand new site?

If your current site has traffic, customers, or search rankings worth keeping, a redesign protects those while fixing structure, speed, and clarity. If the site is very small or the business has changed direction entirely, starting fresh is often cheaper. The blueprint session settles this in about an hour.

Can I update the website myself after launch?

Yes. That is the point. Every build is handed off with a walkthrough so you can edit text, swap images, and publish on your own. You own the domain, the site, and everything in it. No retainers required to touch your own website.

What platforms do you build on?

Whichever fits the business: Framer for most service businesses, Shopify for stores, and hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript when the project needs custom behavior. You are never locked into a platform because it is the only one I sell.

Do you only work with St. Louis businesses?

St. Louis is home and local projects get on-site photography and in-person working sessions. But the process runs fine remotely, and I take select projects from anywhere.
